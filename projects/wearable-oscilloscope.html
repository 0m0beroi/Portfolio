Real‑time signal analysis on your wrist – merging test instrumentation with wearable tech.

Wearables Signal Processing IoT Innovation

Concept

A first‑of‑its‑kind wearable oscilloscope concept enabling engineers and learners to visualize signals in real time without bench equipment. Combines a miniature analog front end, high‑speed sampling and an optimized UI for rapid inspection of embedded prototypes.

Focus areas include ultra‑low power design, miniaturized acquisition circuitry, adaptive sampling and secure wireless streaming for extended analysis on companion devices.

Innovation Highlights

  • Miniature waveform capture & OLED visualization
  • Gesture / touch driven UI interactions
  • Wireless synchronization & logging
  • Battery optimized acquisition modes
